Frequently Asked Questions


When is my credit card charged?

Your credit card is charged once the cleaning is complete.


Can I leave the house during the clean?

Of course! You are welcome to be in or out of your home while the cleaners are there.


Can we book the same cleaner for ongoing cleanings?

Absolutely! You can choose the same cleaner or a new cleaner.


How many cleaners will be cleaning my home?

We typically like to have a minimum of two cleaners for every clean due to staff safety. However, if you have a smaller home, it will be likely that one cleaner will complete the job.


How is the price calculated for each clean?

The price is calculated based on the square footage of your home, number of rooms and bathrooms.


Are your cleaners bonded and insured?

Yes. All of our cleaners are both bonded and insured.
